Amsterdam Energy System

An Exploration of the Amsterdam energy system, current and future.

This webpage was created to provide an overview of the extensive energy grassroots movement in Amsterdam- all around the cities, initiatives are leading the way to a cleaner tomorrow! However, this path comes with many challenges, as well as opportunities, that we can explore together as a united front.
